What It Does

The AI Query takes an unusual signal and a location, and returns a structured summary of what appears to be happening, potential impacts, and what people in the area are doing in response. When relevant, it also surfaces information about roads, transit, and power.

How It Works

You describe what caught your attention and where. That's it.

Lost power, Cambridge MA
Your lights just went out
Strong odor, Midtown Manhattan
You notice an unusual smell
Fire trucks, Shadyside Pittsburgh
Unusual emergency vehicle activity

The query draws from a broad range of sources and applies layered confidence logic — so you're not just getting raw information, you're getting a structured read on how reliable that information appears to be.

This is not advice. It is not a substitute for official communications, emergency services, or your own judgment. AI outputs may be incorrect, incomplete, or fabricated. Use this tool as one input among many — never as your sole basis for action.
